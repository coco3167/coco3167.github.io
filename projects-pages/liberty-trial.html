Liberty Trial


About the game

One player tries to escape a deadly trial as the second one helps him by guiding and deactivating traps in their way.

This game was made during the second year of the Cnam-Enjmin game-programming bachelor's degree.
Liberty Trial was developed in Unreal Engine 5 using network multiplayer between a PC and Mobile player.

My Contributions

I mainly worked on the network and UI part of the game.
I also was responsible for creating the mobile build.
