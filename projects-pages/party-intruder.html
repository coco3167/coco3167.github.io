Party Intruder


About the game

Take on the role of a slasher villain who has targeted an isolated group of people and will seek to kill each of them as creatively as possible without alerting the police.

This game was made during the third year of the Cnam-Enjmin game-programming bachelor's degree.
Party Intruder was developed in Unreal Engine 5 and Perforce as the versioning tool in a team of 7 persons.

My Contributions

I was the Lead Developper on this project, I was mainly responsible for the coordination of the different programmers on the project.
The weapon system was one of my main focus during this project.
I also created the main code-architecture of the project using C++ at first.
